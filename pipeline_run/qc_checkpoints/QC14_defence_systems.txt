KEY BIOLOGICAL FINDINGS
========================
1. Rich, multi-layered defence landscape in both microbiomes:
   CRC: 208 DF + 511 PADLOC systems; Control: 371 DF + 913 PADLOC systems
   Per-bin rates comparable after excluding chimeric CRC bin.19 (101 systems alone)

2. PADLOC reveals large novel defence repertoire (PDC-* systems):
   CRC 251/511 (49%), Control 410/913 (45%) are PADLOC-specific novel systems
   PDC-S01 is most prevalent in both (76 CRC, 99 Control)

3. CRC bin.19 defence island (chimeric artefact):
   101 DefenseFinder systems in one chimeric bin (contamination=102.7%, 37 Mb)
   Treat independently; likely co-assembled fragments from multiple organisms

4. Cas9 (Class2-II-A) in CRC bin.18:
   CRISPR arrays confirmed by CRISPRCasFinder → complete, functional CRISPR-Cas9 locus
   Class I-E also in bin.24 with CRISPR arrays

5. Greater Cas subtype diversity in control (11 vs 5 subtypes):
   Control dominated by Class 1-I-C (17×) — the most common gut bacterial CRISPR system
   CRC shows reduced but maintained CRISPR-Cas repertoire

6. Abortive infection systems reduced in CRC:
   AbiD: 0 (CRC) vs 18+22 (Control, DF+PADLOC); AbiQ: 0 vs 8
   Suggests altered phage-host arms race dynamics in CRC dysbiosis

7. CBASS cyclic-nucleotide signalling unique to CRC (4 DF):
   CBASS absent in control → potentially CRC-associated organism marker
   (Consistent with enrichment of specific pathogens like F. nucleatum, pks+ E. coli)

8. Control has broader PADLOC repertoire:
   137 vs 100 unique system types; 913 vs 511 instances
   Reflects 3× more bins (92 vs 30) AND a more diverse microbiome
