=== BIOLOGICAL INTERPRETATION ===
The mobilome profile is consistent with a VLP-enriched sample:
1. The 124 plasmid contigs are likely small circular DNAs co-purified with VLPs
   (plasmids <10 kb are often mistaken for or co-pellet with phage particles)
2. The 16 IS elements are distributed across bins that also contain metaviralspades
   contigs — most likely representing bacterial DNA co-assembled with phage
3. Zero integrons confirms the phage-dominated nature of these bins
4. The mobilome from actual bacterial metagenome MAGs (TCGA/Hartwig tumor WGS)
   would be expected to show substantially higher IS, integron, and ICE content,
   particularly for known oncogenic bacteria (H. pylori, E. coli B2)
